== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Once activated, you'll find Quinn in the WordPress admin menu, Click on it, You will be asked to enter your email,
your website url and password to create an account on Quinn.
4. Once account has been created, You will be asked to go to Quinn dashboard at https://newadmin.quinn.live/ and create your first video.
5. If you have any questions, please reach out to us at admin@quinn.live
6. Once you have uploaded videos and created a carousel or story widget, you can go to edit your theme section and add Quinn Carousel or Quinn Story block to your page, Save the page and
you will see the carousel or story widget on your page.
